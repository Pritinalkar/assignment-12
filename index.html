CHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ



Biography of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chatrapti Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19th February 1630,in the village of shivneri, near Pune.His father's name was Shahaji Bhosale,and his mother's name was Jijabai.

Family Background

Shivaji Maharaj was born into a family belonging to the Maratha caste,which had risen from being one among the lower castes in society to becoming rulers of an independent kingdom by the mid-eighteenth century under the leadership of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj,who established his rule at Raigad fort in Maharashtra.
